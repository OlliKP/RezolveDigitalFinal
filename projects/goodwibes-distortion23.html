DISTORTION x GOODWIBES

Distortion Backyard Party / Lineup Animation

The Assignment:

The client entrusted us with the task of producing an animated video that effectively presented the lineup for their stage at Distortion Festival 2023.

A fellow designer had previously established a robust visual identity, which served as the basis for our creative development.

The client's vision encompassed an energetic and vibrant video that effectively encapsulates the immersive atmosphere of the event, conveying the distinct vibe experienced by attendees, while simultaneously providing clear visibility of the performing artists.
